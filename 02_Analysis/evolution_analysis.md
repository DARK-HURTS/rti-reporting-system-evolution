# Evolution of the RTI Annual Reporting System (2021–2025)

---

## 2021 — Baseline (Original System)

### Key Characteristics

**1. Basic Compliance Template**  
Focused mainly on:
- Applications  
- Trainings  
- Challenges  
- Recommendations  

Satisfied legal requirements but provided limited operational insight  

**2. Weak Data Structure**  
- Mixed quantitative and qualitative inputs  
- No standardized format  

Difficult to aggregate data at a national level  

**3. Missing Critical Dimensions**  
No tracking of:
- Request types  
- Response timelines  
- Applicant demographics  
- Access channels  

Limited analytical and policy value  

**4. Minimal Accountability**  
- No responsible officer tracking  
- No validation fields  

Weak audit trail  

---

## 2022 — Minor Structural Improvement

### Improvements Introduced

**1. Metadata Addition**
- Generated by  
- Position  
- Contact details  

Introduced basic accountability  

### Limitations

- Core structure unchanged  
- Still semi-structured and text-heavy  

Not yet data-ready  

---

## 2023 — Major System Redesign

### Key Enhancements

**1. Shift to Requests-Based System**
- Requests received  
- Partially granted  
- Review outcomes  

Enabled lifecycle tracking  

**2. Data Segmentation**
- Applicant biodata (age, gender)  

Enabled demographic analysis  

**3. Structured Training Categories**
- Staff sensitization  
- Officer training  

Improved clarity and evaluation  

**4. Enhanced Information Manual Tracking**
- Compilation vs publication  

Introduced progress tracking  

**5. Expanded Standardization**
- Clearly separated challenges and recommendations  

Improved policy feedback  

### Key Insight

Transition from narrative reporting to structured data collection  

---

## 2024 — Standardization & Governance

### Major Improvements

**1. Form-Based Structure**
- Restricted editing  
- Controlled input  

Prevented data inconsistencies and loss  

**2. Authentication & Validation**
- Prepared by  
- Head of institution  
- Signature and seal  

Strengthened accountability  

**3. Application Channels Introduced**
- Written  
- Oral  
- In-person  
- Electronic  

Enabled service delivery analysis  

**4. Standardized Responses**
- YES/NO fields  

Improved data quality  

### Key Insight

Transition from data collection to controlled data input system  

---

## 2025 — Analytics & Compliance Maturity

### Enhancements

**1. Performance Metrics**
- Average response time  
- Internal review timelines  

Enabled performance measurement  

**2. Expanded Information Manual Assessment**
- Availability  
- Publication  
- Accessibility  
- Updates  

Multi-dimensional compliance tracking  

**3. Records Management Tracking**
- Central register  
- Filing system  
- Response tracking  

Institutional maturity assessment  

**4. Improved Logical Flow**
- Reorganized sections  

Improved usability  

### Key Insight

Transition to a national monitoring and evaluation tool  

---

## Key Insights Across Evolution

- Shift from narrative reporting → structured data system  
- Introduction of governance and validation controls  
- Progressive enhancement toward analytics readiness  
- Increased standardization across institutions  

---

## Core Contributions

**1. Standardization**
- Eliminated inconsistent reporting  
- Introduced structured formats  

**2. Data Integrity Control**
- Restricted editing  
- Prevented data loss and manipulation  

**3. Analytics Enablement**
- Introduced categories, metrics, and structured inputs  
- Enabled national-level analysis and reporting  

---

## Outcome

- Improved measurement of RTI compliance across institutions  
- Enabled richer data for analysis and reporting  
- Strengthened national-level visibility of implementation
